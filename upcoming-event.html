Enter the Ghost Town
System: D&D 5e | Seats: 6 | GM: Chol (Sounds like 'Cole')
Experience Level: No Experience Required
Around a century ago, the town of Midford vanished without a trace. No warnings, no survivors. One day it stood as a thriving mining settlement, built upon rich veins of gold. The next, it was simply gone. Despite countless searches, no explanation has ever been found.
Now, just as mysteriously as it disappeared, Midford has returned… albeit, without any of its previously living occupants.
The town’s sudden reappearance has sparked a frenzy amongst those seeking fortune: merchants, prospectors, miners, even scholars. All hoping to uncover its secrets… or at least profit from them. Amongst these ambitious souls is the enterprising Reginald Alabaster, who is seeking capable adventurers to help clear and secure his new premises.
Whether drawn by the promise of gold, the lure of ancient mysteries, or the opportunity to earn the favor of a well-connected patron, you and your companions have answered the call. With Midford’s gates open once more after a century of absence, who can say what wonders – or dangers – await within?
Will you be among the first to uncover the secrets of the lost town?
Tales from the Badger Den is an organized play community (similar in style to Adventurer’s League) based in and around Madison. All of the content is written by local members. This module is one such adventure from the Kingdom of Stonewell region. Feel free to talk to Chol for more information.
