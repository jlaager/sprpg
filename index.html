Sun Prairie RPG Night

A free, community-focused tabletop RPG group dedicated to helping people learn new games, meet fellow gamers, and find groups to play with.

Whether you're a longtime player or completely new to tabletop roleplaying games, you're welcome at our table.

What to Expect

  • Free tabletop RPG events
  • Beginner-friendly games and opportunities to learn new systems
  • Pregenerated characters are provided for most games
  • A welcoming, inclusive tabletop community for adults
